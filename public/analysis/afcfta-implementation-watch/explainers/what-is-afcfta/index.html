What Is AfCFTA?

AfCFTA is a continental trade agreement designed to increase trade among African countries. The practical question is implementation: which rules are usable, which institutions are ready, and which businesses can actually trade under the framework.

Why it matters

This concept affects whether AfCFTA can move from policy language into usable trade practice.

What readers should check

Look for the country, product, institution, date, rule, and official guidance behind any specific claim.

Afrodescendant Ali lens

The focus is practical Black World intelligence: trade readiness, institutional capacity, diaspora capital, and implementation evidence.
