AfCFTA Findings Register

This page separates working findings from open questions. Findings summarize what the public watch currently treats as usable framing for readers.

Finding 1

AfCFTA is best understood as an implementation challenge, not only a treaty announcement or market-size headline.

Finding 2

Rules of origin, standards, border processes, payments, and logistics determine whether businesses can use the agreement in practice.

Finding 3

MSMEs and diaspora-linked businesses need practical information before they can convert AfCFTA attention into trade decisions.
