Afro-Europe

This lane reads Black Europe through policy, migration, colonial memory, media representation, anti-racism institutions, and Africa-Europe relations.

What Ali tracks

Policy, memory, migration, and media.

Coverage follows EU anti-racism policy, national action plans, UK/France/Portugal/Netherlands dynamics, migration, policing, media representation, colonial memory, and Africa-Europe relations.

Why it matters

Europe is a Black-world power center.

European institutions influence migration, development finance, African policy, cultural memory, and global racial discourse. Afro-Europe belongs inside the strategic map.
